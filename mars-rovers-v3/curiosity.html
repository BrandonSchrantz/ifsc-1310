The Mars Rovers

What a wonder the reaches of space are. Exspecially the red planet, Mars. Many people have often wondered what sights that planet has to present, but for the longest time, our eyes could only get as close to it through a telescopic lense. But curiosity of this world, one more similar to Earth than any other planet in the solar system, would not be stymied by a little distance of 140 million miles. So we launched unmanned rovers, and they actually made there! But what did these rovers find and discoveries they make? What technologies did they employ? And what exactly was their target objective?

Meet the Rovers

While there are a number of unmaned robotics that have been sent to mars, in total there have been five NASA rovers that have landed on this "new" strange world that has only recently been brought within reach of humanity.
