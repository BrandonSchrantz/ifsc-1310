Mission

This rover was part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory mission. Too this day, it is still active. What is its purpose through? Something quite remarkable to imagine. Its task was to determine if their was life on mars. Specifically microbial life. Now lets discuss how it could be possible for this noble rover to complete this objective, when it set off on its journey, its remarkable ability to follow up its ancestors and land on Mars, and what it did when it got there.
